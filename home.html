Kappa Kappa Psi MIssion Statement
Kappa Kappa Psi is a fraternal organization that promotes the advancement of college and university bands through dedicated service and support to bands; comprehensive education; leadership opportunities; and recognition; for the benefit of its members and society.

Day of Mourning--posted August 27, 2004

Brothers and Sisters,

It is with a heavy heart that I inform you of recent losses in our KKY/TBS family. Steven McLemore, Publications Manager for Kappa Kappa Psi & Tau Beta Sigma has passed away. Also,the Texas Tech chapters Alpha Omicron (KKY) and Beta (TBS) lost Sisters JT Turner & Sabrina Turner Coffman, and Brother Adam Coffman in a tragic car accident.

In memory of our lost Brothers and Sisters, all actives are requested to wear the official Badge of Mourning on Monday, August 30th, 2004, which has been designated an offical Day of Mourning for KKY/TBS.

The Badge of Mourning for both organizations consists of taking a piece of black ribbon, 5/8" wide, and folding it into an inverted "V" shape and pinning your COFA (official memberhsip pin) through the middle. [Note: if wearing the Rec. Bar, it is pinned through the center of the ribbon without folding it.] More information can be found in the "Guide to Membership Education" book.

We ask that all Brothers and Sisters nationwide keep the families of Steven, JT, Sabrina, and Adam in their prayers.

 

AEA, and Rest in Peace.
