The Delta Chapter of Kappa Kappa Psi

University of Oklahoma

Est. 1921

Kappa Kappa Psi is a national honorary band fraternity, and the Delta chapter is the University of Oklahoma's chapter. Our organization, along with the Delta chapter of Tau Beta Sigma, the national honorary band sorority, supports our university's various musical ensembles and the surrounding community, while having a lot of fun ourselves. We hope that you enjoy checking our chapter out!
